About us

Welden School Mbarara is a private, day and boarding school for O' and A' levels.
Welden School Mbarara was founded in 2021 and has since been a beacon of academic excellence. The school is located in uganda, Mbarara district western region, katete cell, Nyamitanga
The idea to start the school was born out of the need to help the less fortunate children in the region to access education. It is registered with Ministry of Education and Sports.

Vision

To be at the helm of producing competent human resource to serve the church, state and the world.

Mission

To provide quality education through practical skills, teamwork, self reliance and produce God fearing persons.

Core Values

Fear God;

Integrity;

Respect of persons and property

UCE 2024 Results: The school registered 91 candidates for the UNEB UCE 2024 exams and achieved remarkable results with all 89 students scoring a grade of 1.
Sports Achievements: Welden School Mbarara has made history by winning its first-ever Uganda Secondary Schools Sports Association (USSSA) Mbarara City football title, ending a 21-year wait for glory.
Football Champion: The school has been crowned the undisputed champion of the Uganda Secondary Schools Sports Association (USSSA) Ankole zone boys’ football.
Welden School Mbarara continues to strive for excellence in education and sports, contributing to the growth and development of its students and the community.
