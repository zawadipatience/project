CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

sports & athletics

Welden School Mbarara promotes sports and athletics to develop students’ talents, teamwork, and discipline. Learners participate in football, volleyball, basketball, athletics, and other indoor and outdoor games to promote physical fitness and competition.

clubs & societies

The school offers various clubs and societies to develop students’ skills and creativity. These include Debate Club, ICT Club, Wildlife Club, Scripture Union, Science Club, Journalism Club, and Music, Dance and Drama.

leadership & services

Welden School Mbarara nurtures leadership skills by giving students opportunities to serve as prefects, class monitors, and club leaders. This helps learners develop responsibility, confidence, and teamwork skills.

OUR ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

O'level

The O'level academic programme at Welden School Mbarara is designed to provide a strong and balanced foundation across all key subjects. Through a well-structured curriculum, experienced teachers, and continuous assessment, we equip learners with the knowledge, skills, and discipline needed to excel in national examinations and progress confidently to higher levels of education.

We offer a four year program that covers both sciences and arts subjects according to the uganda national curriculum .

  • Core subjects: English and Mathematics.
  • Sciences: Biology, Physics, chemistry, Agriculture, ICT, PE.
  • Humanities: History, Geography, Kiswahili, CRE, Entrepreneurship, Literature in English, Runyankole, Fine Art.

A'level

Our A'Level programme offers specialized subject combinations that prepare students for higher education and career pathways. With a focus on in-depth understanding, research skills, and independent learning, we equip learners to excel in national examinations and compete effectively at university level and beyond.

A two year advanced program with combinations in sciences like BCM, PCM, PCB, PEM etc

  • Physics, Chemistry,Biology
  • Mathematics,Economics
  • Agriculture, ICT

A two year advanced program with combinations in Arts like HEG, HAD, LEA, HAG etc

  • History, Geography,Entrepreneurship
  • literature,Economics, Divinity
  • ICT
